Beef Tacos with Wasabi Mayo

Mex meets Japanese flavor for one rich taco.

There is never enough...

For The Meat

  • 2 beef sirloin steaks
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • olive oil
  • 6-8 small corn tortillas

For The Marinade

  • light miso paste
  • mirin
  • sugar
  • olive oil
  • sea salt and freshly ground pepper

For The Quick Pickled Onions

  • onion
  • sea salt and freshly ground pepper
  • rice vinegar or lemon juice
  • toasted sesame oil
  • dried chile flakes

For The Wasabi Mayo

  • wasabi
  • mayonnaise

The Process:

  1. Buy Gordon Ramsay's Home Cooking Book
  2. Read the recipe
  3. Believe you can cook like Ramsay
  4. Cook one of the best meals of your life
  5. Cry because you know Gordon's is that much better
  6. Try his next great recipe
